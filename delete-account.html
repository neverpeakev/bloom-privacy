Delete Your Bloom Account

We're sorry to see you go. If you'd like to delete your Bloom: Menopause Tracker App account and all associated data, you can do so using one of the methods below.

Delete from Within the App

  1. Open the Bloom app on your device
  2. Go to Settings (gear icon in the top right)
  3. Tap Account
  4. Tap Delete Account
  5. Confirm your decision when prompted

Request Deletion via Email

If you are unable to delete your account through the app, you can request account deletion by emailing us at kevin@neverpeakmarketing.com with the subject line "Account Deletion Request" and the email address associated with your Bloom account.

What Gets Deleted

When you delete your account, the following data will be permanently removed within 30 days:

  1. Your account profile and login credentials
  2. All symptom tracking data (hot flashes, night sweats, mood, sleep, etc.)
  3. Any community posts or comments
  4. App preferences and settings

Data Retention

After deletion, we may retain anonymized, aggregated data that cannot be linked back to you for analytics purposes. No personally identifiable information is kept after account deletion is processed.

Contact Us

If you have questions about account deletion, contact us at kevin@neverpeakmarketing.com.
