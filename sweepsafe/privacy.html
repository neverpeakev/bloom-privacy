Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 15, 2026

The short version: SweepSafe runs entirely on your device. Camera scans, sensor readings, sweeps and findings never leave your phone. No accounts, no ads, no trackers.

1. Camera & sensors

The scanner uses your camera, and the sensor tools use the magnetometer and Bluetooth, only on your device, in real time. No images, video, or sensor data are recorded, stored off-device, or transmitted to us or anyone else.

2. Information we collect

No personal information — no name, email, contacts, photos, or location. There are no accounts. Your sweeps, settings and any findings you log are stored locally on your device and can be erased anytime in More → Reset.

3. Purchases

SweepSafe is free with an optional Pro subscription. Payments are processed by Apple; our subscription provider (RevenueCat) processes purchase status using an anonymous identifier — never your name, email or payment details.

4. Ads & third parties

No advertising, no analytics/tracking SDKs. RevenueCat is used solely to manage subscriptions.

5. Intended use

SweepSafe is a personal-safety and counter-surveillance tool for checking spaces you occupy. It is not for surveilling others. No detection method can guarantee a space is free of devices.
