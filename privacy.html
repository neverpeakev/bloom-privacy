Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 10, 2026

The short version: WorldCopa collects no personal information — no names, emails, photos or contacts, no accounts, no ads, and no advertising trackers. Your child's progress stays on the device. The only data involved is what's needed to deliver a subscription you choose to buy.

1. Who we are

WorldCopa ("the app") is an educational flag-coloring and geography game designed for children and families during the FIFA World Cup 2026 season.

2. Information we collect

No personal information — no names, email addresses, photos, contacts or location. The app has no accounts and no login. The only data processed is purchase information if you choose to subscribe (see "Purchases" below), handled through Apple and our subscription provider using an anonymous identifier that is not tied to your name.

3. Local progress data

Game progress (colored flags, stars, badges, streaks and settings) is saved using your browser's local storage, on your device only. It never leaves the device and is never sent to us or anyone else. You can erase it at any time from Grown-ups corner → Data controls → Reset all progress, or by clearing your browser data.

4. Children's privacy (COPPA)

The app is designed for children under 13 in line with COPPA principles: no collection of personal data, no behavioural advertising, no social features or open chat, and a grown-ups gate in front of purchase and data-control screens.

5. Ads and third parties

The app shows no advertising and embeds no ad networks, analytics trackers, or social SDKs. To offer optional subscriptions, the app uses RevenueCat, a subscription-management service, solely to process and restore purchases. RevenueCat receives an anonymous app-generated identifier and your purchase status — never your name, email, or contact details. See RevenueCat's privacy policy at revenuecat.com/privacy.

6. Purchases

WorldCopa is free to use with an optional WorldCopa Pro subscription (and a one-time Lifetime option). All payments are processed exclusively by Apple through the App Store, behind a parental gate. Apple and our subscription provider (RevenueCat) process the purchase using an anonymous identifier only — we never see or store payment details or personal information.

7. Changes to this policy

If the app's data practices ever change, this page will be updated and the "last updated" date revised before the changes take effect.

8. Contact

Questions? Reach the team at privacy@worldcopa.example (demo placeholder).
