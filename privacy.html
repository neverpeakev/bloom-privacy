Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 10, 2026

The short version: Flag Explorer 2026 collects no personal information at all. There are no accounts, no ads, no trackers and no third-party analytics. Everything your child does in the app stays on your device.

1. Who we are

Flag Explorer 2026 ("the app") is an educational flag-coloring and geography game designed for children and families during the FIFA World Cup 2026 season.

2. Information we collect

None. The app does not ask for, collect, store or transmit any personal information — no names, email addresses, photos, contacts, location or device identifiers.

3. Local progress data

Game progress (colored flags, stars, badges, streaks and settings) is saved using your browser's local storage, on your device only. It never leaves the device and is never sent to us or anyone else. You can erase it at any time from Grown-ups corner → Data controls → Reset all progress, or by clearing your browser data.

4. Children's privacy (COPPA)

The app is designed for children under 13 in line with COPPA principles: no personal data collection, no behavioural advertising, no social features or open chat, and a grown-ups gate in front of plan and data-control screens.

5. Ads and third parties

The app shows no advertising and embeds no third-party SDKs, trackers, or analytics.

6. Purchases

This product preview operates in demo mode: no real payments are collected. Any future paid version will process payments exclusively through the platform's store (e.g. App Store / Google Play) behind a parental gate, and this policy will be updated first.

7. Changes to this policy

If the app's data practices ever change, this page will be updated and the "last updated" date revised before the changes take effect.

8. Contact

Questions? Reach the team at privacy@flagexplorer.example (demo placeholder).
