Color the Cup. Learn the World.

WorldCopa turns the excitement of the 2026 World Cup into the most fun geography lesson your kids will ever ask for. Color every nation's flag, master quick-fire trivia, and explore the real tournament — all in one bright, friendly app built for kids, families, and classrooms.

★ COLOR ALL 48 FLAGS
Paint every qualified nation's flag, region by region, in a guided mode that gently teaches the real colors — or free-paint mode for pure creativity. Each finished flag unlocks a fun fact and a burst of confetti.

★ PLAY & LEARN
• Trivia Arena — capitals, continents, languages and football lore, with a friendly explanation after every answer
• Flag Match — spot the right flag before the clock runs out
• Continent Quest — sort countries onto the right continent
• Daily Challenge — a fresh quiz every day to keep your streak alive

★ FOLLOW THE REAL TOURNAMENT
All 48 teams, 12 groups and the full 104-match schedule across Canada, Mexico and the USA — plus a Matchday Challenge built from each day's actual fixtures.

★ FILL YOUR PASSPORT
Earn stars, collect country stamps, unlock badges and build daily streaks. Progress that makes kids want to keep learning.

★ BUILT FOR KIDS & FAMILIES
• No ads, no trackers, no accounts — ever
• Works fully offline
• A grown-ups area behind a parental gate
